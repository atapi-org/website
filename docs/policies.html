Membership Service

Sponsors may contact atari@atapi.org at any time to request assistance with their membership.

Consultation Policy

Monthly consultations, in cases where they are provided as a component of sponsorship, are not available during months when researcher-subjects are either on course or in seclusion. Monthly consultations not taken within the scope of any given calendar month are considered forfeit. Missed monthly consultations do not roll over to the following month and are not cumulative.

Cancellation Policy

Sponsor memberships are billed monthly. Sponsors may cancel their membership at any time.

Refund and Dispute Policy

Sponsor memberships are billed continuously (monthly). Under normal circumstances, refunds will not be provided. If a sponsorship payment is made in error, a refund for that month's payment can be requested by emailing atari@atapi.org. Due to the nature of the research conducted by Atapi Research Inc., please allow up to 90 calendar days for a refund to be processed.
